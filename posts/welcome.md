# Welcome to my corner of the web

I've wanted a single place to put the things I care about — and here it is.

This site brings together a few threads of my life:

- **Dentistry** — the work I trained for and love.
- **Public health** — where I volunteer, and where I've learned to think beyond the chair.
- **Writing** — short notes to help me think clearly.

## Why a blog?

Writing forces me to slow down. When I explain something in plain words, I find
out how well I actually understand it. So expect posts on what I'm learning in
public health, reflections from clinical training, and my journey toward a DDS.

> "The palest ink is better than the best memory."

## What's next

I'll be adding posts as I go — and photographs a little later on. If something
here resonates, the [contact page](../contact.html) is always open.

Thanks for reading. 🌿
