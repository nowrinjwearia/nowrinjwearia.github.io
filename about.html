A little about me

A dentist at heart, driven toward more

I'm an internationally trained dentist. I earned my BDS from Chittagong Medical College and spent a year as an intern doctor, caring for patients across core dental and medical departments. Since then I've passed the INBDE and shadowed dentists here in the U.S. — every step grounded in one belief: good dentistry is evidence-based and deeply human.

Beyond the chair, I volunteer in public health at UC Berkeley. Working with data and communities — I've co-authored a peer-reviewed study and grown comfortable in R and Stata — has shown me how much of oral health is decided long before a patient ever sits down. Closing those gaps is the work I care about.

My next step is an advanced-standing DDS, where I can sharpen my clinical skills and care for the diverse communities that need them most. When I'm not studying, you'll usually find me behind a camera. This site is where all of that comes together.
